Unity 2D Plane Asset Pack (No Red Star Version)

Files are separated into individual PNGs with transparent backgrounds where possible.

Suggested Unity import settings:
- Texture Type: Sprite (2D and UI)
- Sprite Mode: Single for individual files
- Pixels Per Unit: 100
- Filter Mode: Point or Bilinear depending on your game style
- Compression: None or Low Quality for clearer edges
